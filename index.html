FAQ

What is an eSIM?
An eSIM is a digital SIM built into modern smartphones. Scan a QR code to activate — no physical SIM needed.
Is my device compatible?
iPhone XS+, Pixel 3+, Samsung Galaxy S20+, and most modern phones support eSIM.
Can I keep my regular SIM?
Yes. Most devices support dual SIM — keep your regular SIM for calls while using eSIM for data.
Standard vs Unlimited?
Standard plans have a fixed data allowance. Unlimited plans offer uncapped data for a set number of days.
Which countries are covered?
30+ countries including France, Germany, Italy, Spain, UK, Netherlands, Switzerland, Portugal, Greece, Austria, and more.
What if my data runs out?
Your connection stops. Purchase a top-up or new plan. No surprise charges with prepaid plans.
