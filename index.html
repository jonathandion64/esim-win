Frequently asked

What is an eSIM?
An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. Scan a QR code to activate — no physical card, no airport kiosk.
Is my phone compatible?
iPhone XS+, Pixel 3+, Samsung S20+, and most modern phones support eSIM. Check Settings → Mobile Data → Add eSIM.
How fast is delivery?
Instant — your QR code arrives in your inbox seconds after purchase. Install it before you travel.
Can I keep my regular SIM?
Yes — dual SIM lets you keep your home number for calls and use the eSIM for data abroad.
What if my data runs out?
Your connection pauses — no surprise charges. Top up or buy a new plan anytime.
