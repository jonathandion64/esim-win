Frequently asked

What is an eSIM?
An eSIM is a digital SIM built into your phone. Scan a QR code to activate — no physical card, no local SIM shop needed.
Is my device compatible?
iPhone XS+, Pixel 3+, Samsung Galaxy S20+ and most modern phones support eSIM. Check Settings → Mobile Data → Add eSIM.
Can I keep my regular SIM?
Yes — dual SIM lets you keep your home number for calls while using the eSIM for data in Saint Lucia.
What if my data runs out?
Your connection pauses — no surprise charges. Buy a top-up or new plan anytime.
